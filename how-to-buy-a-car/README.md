# How to Buy a Car

This folder contains information, research, and guides related to purchasing a vehicle.

## Topics to Cover

- Research and preparation
- Budget planning
- New vs. used cars
- Car models comparison
- Financing options
- Insurance considerations
- Inspection checklist
- Negotiation strategies
- Legal documents and paperwork
- Maintenance plans

## How to Use

Document your car-buying journey and research. For example:
- `research/` - Car model comparisons and reviews
- `budget-planning.md` - Your budget breakdown
- `inspection-checklist.md` - What to check when viewing a car
- `negotiation-tips.md` - Strategies for getting a good deal
- `financing-options.md` - Loan and payment options
