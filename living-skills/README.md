# Living Skills

This folder contains practical life skills, how-to guides, and useful tips for everyday living.

## Example Topics

- Cooking recipes and techniques
- Home maintenance tips
- Financial management
- DIY projects
- Cleaning and organization
- Basic repairs
- Budgeting strategies
- Shopping tips

## How to Use

Create guides and notes for different life skills. For example:
- `cooking/` - Recipes and cooking techniques
- `home-maintenance/` - Tips for maintaining your home
- `financial/` - Money management strategies
- `organization/` - Organization systems and tips

## Template

```markdown
# [Skill/Task Name]

**Category**: [e.g., Cooking, Finance, Maintenance]
**Difficulty**: [Easy/Medium/Hard]
**Time Required**: [Estimated time]

## What You'll Need

- Item 1
- Item 2

## Steps

1. Step 1
2. Step 2
3. Step 3

## Tips & Tricks

- Tip 1
- Tip 2

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Mistake 1
- Mistake 2

## Resources

- [Helpful link](url)
```
